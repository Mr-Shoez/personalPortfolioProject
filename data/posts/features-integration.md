> [!CAUTION] Integration Hell
> Dropping a shiny new feature into an existing monolithic codebase often breaks three other unrelated things. Identifying the blast radius of a new integration is terrifying in a large repo.

> [!TIP] Feature Flags & Strangler Fig
> Utilize feature flags to conditionally roll out the new integration. Pair this with the Strangler Fig pattern where you slowly route traffic to the new microservice instead of doing a risky "Big Bang" release.
